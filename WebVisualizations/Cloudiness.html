Latitude vs Cloudiness

Cloudiness (also known as Cloud cover, cloudage, or cloud amount) refers to the fraction of the sky obscured by clouds when observed from a particular location. Cloudiness is correlated to the sunshine duration as the least cloudy locales are the sunniest ones while the cloudiest areas are the least sunny places.


Summary


There are large groups of cities with 0% and 100% cloudiness between latitudes of -40 and 80 however there is no significant correlation between latitude and cloudiness.
